# DIALECTICA VC Demo Script

Use `/situation-demo` as the first page. Do not start in GraphOps.

## 60-Second Talk Track

1. "DIALECTICA turns messy conflict information into an evidence-backed graph."
2. "The input can be a policy briefing, a book, an investigation file, or internal notes."
3. "The system extracts the situation: actors, events, interests, constraints, evidence, and causal links."
4. "Now the user can ask questions a normal chatbot cannot answer reliably, because the answer is grounded in graph paths and rules."
5. "For Syria, we can ask whether Russia's 2015 intervention caused regime survival. The answer is not just a paragraph. It shows the causal chain and the evidence."
6. "That same graph backbone powers policy analysis, mediation, legal strategy, risk dashboards, and TACITUS products."
7. "The wedge is conflict intelligence. The platform is structured reasoning over high-stakes human systems."

## Click Path

1. Open `/`.
2. Click `See the 90-second demo`.
3. On `/situation-demo`, explain the three-step flow:
   - read the situation;
   - build the graph;
   - ask real questions.
4. Click `Open the answer demo`.
5. On `/demo/syria/reasoning`, show:
   - question library;
   - LLM comparison;
   - DIALECTICA traced answer;
   - graph citation path;
   - trace drawer JSON.
6. If time allows, click `/demo` to show ingestion theatre.

## What To Avoid

- Do not start with GraphOps, Databricks, Cozo, Graphiti, or internal pipeline details.
- Do not explain every ontology type.
- Do not say "AI agent platform" first.
- Do not over-index on Syria. Syria is the example; the product is the graph backbone.

## Core Message

"LLMs summarize text. DIALECTICA structures situations. Once a situation is structured, every TACITUS product can reason over it with evidence, time, causality, and provenance."
